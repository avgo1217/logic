How to Optimally Analyze Progress with the Logic Tool

Written by Coach Moby - N1 Esports


Optimal practice and analysis

  • Choose 1-2 playlists with 5-10 scenarios to practice for a set training block (ex. 1 month).
  • Ensure that your playlists include scenarios that are custom tailored for you as an individual and well rounded (including clicking, tracking, switching, etc).
  • Play that playlist for 20-60 minutes a day. If you have an unusually bad day, call it early and put your focus into something else and try again tomorrow.
  • Upload your data to Logic weekly (ex. Sunday at 9am)
  • Analyze your progress in each scenario and set goals for yourself to achieve.
  • Restructure your practice each week/month to compensate for strengths/weaknesses.

Dos and Don'ts

Dos

  • Upload weekly and analyze weekly trends in progress - pick a day each week where you will upload your data and look at your stats for your top 5-10 scenarios.
  • Analyze monthly progressions - when developing any skill, you will make strong progress initially and then progression will slow. The more advanced you are the more you should be looking at long term trends. It is important to see how far you have come in order to keep a positive habit.
  • Practice often and in short bursts.

Don'ts

  • Upload and analyze data daily - you will have good days and bad days and this will only get you inside your head if you are constantly criticizing yourself for a bad day or over celebrating a random high score that is not representative of overall progression
  • Practice more than 1-2 hours total per day
  • Practice a single scenario for more than 20 minutes straight
