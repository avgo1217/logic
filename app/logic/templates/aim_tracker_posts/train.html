What is Kovaak’s FPS Aim Trainer and How Can You Use It to Improve?

Written by Coach Moby - N1 Esports


What is an Aim Trainer?

An aim trainer is a game/tool used to isolate and practice aim mechanics by eliminating other factors common in mainstream games.


Why use an aim trainer?

Aim trainers increase your aiming uptime. When playing a standard game of Fortnite or Valorant, you spend a large percent of your time looting or running around without firing your weapon. Aim trainers, on the other hand, allow you to isolate that skill and practice it with near 100% uptime.

This will allow you to improve your aiming mechanics faster than you ever could playing your main game.


Why use Kovaak's FPS Aim Trainer?

There are numerous FPS aim trainers on the market and Kovaak’s is only one of your options.

The first thing you should look for when picking an FPS aim trainer is one that is played in a 3D space. 2D aim trainers do not transfer as well to 3D games like Fortnite, Valorant, CS GO, Overwatch, etc.

The next thing you should look for is customization and depth of options. Kovaak’s has a wealth of options that enable you to change your sensitivity, FOV, UI, crosshair, textures, colors, etc to match whatever game you want to emulate.

Kovaak’s also features over 3,000 scenarios that allow you to isolate specific aspects of your aim whether it be clicking, tracking, target switching or strafing. This is accomplished by changing the size of targets, the speed at which they move, their health, how far away they are and many other variables within each individual scenario


How can you use Kovaak's to improve?

Due to the overwhelming amount of community developed scenarios within Kovaak’s, it can be difficult to know what scenarios to practice, how long to practice (volume) and how often to practice (frequency). As a result, many users waste their time playing scenarios like Jumbo Tile Frenzy for hours.

In order to structure your practice more intelligently, I recommend you build a well rounded playlist within Kovaak’s and keep practicing a set of 5-10 scenarios to more accurately access your progress over time instead of playing random scenarios every time you log in.

You can find good playlist recommendations on our Logic playlist page, on the Sparky discord (hyperlink), in the Aimer7 guide (hyperlink) or within the default playlists on Kovaak’s.
