Introduction to FPS Aim Training

Written by Coach Moby - N1 Esports


What is Raw Aim?

Raw aim is the mechanical ability an athlete possesses in relation to mouse control. This includes clicking, tracking and target switching and excludes any component of game sense, crosshair placement, prediction, reaction, etc.


Types of Raw Aim

Clicking: The ability to hit targets with single clicks.

Example Kovaak’s scenarios:

  • 1wall6targets TE
  • 1wall6targets small
  • 1w2ts reload
  • 1w6ts reload
  • 1wall5targets_pasu
  • Tile Frenzy
  • Tile Frenzy 180 Strafing

Tracking: The ability to keep one’s crosshair on a moving target.

Example Kovaak’s scenarios:

  • Close long strafes (invincible)
  • Vertical long strafes
  • Thin aiming long invincible
  • Air Invincible
  • Close fast strafes (invincible)
  • Midrange Long Strafes Invincible
  • FuglaaXYLongstrafes

Switching: The ability to transition one’s crosshair from tracking target A to tracking target B.

Example Kovaak’s scenarios:

  • Bounce 180 Tracking
  • patTargetSwitch
  • patTargetSwitch small
  • Tile Frenzy 180 Tracking
  • Tile Frenzy 180 Tracking 200%

Mechanical Components of Raw Aim

Accuracy: Shots hit divided by shots fired. Ability to adjust crosshair to a target location with precision.

Precision: Ability to replicate a shot on a target location repeatedly.

Visual Reaction Time: Interval of time between presentation of stimulus and appearance of appropriate voluntary response.

Speed: The speed at which one adjusts aim from target to target.

Smoothness: Ability to move one’s crosshair in a direction with a consistent velocity.

Pathing: The path your crosshair follows when adjusting from target A to target B.


Mental Components of Raw Aim

Reading: Ability to analyze and interpret current target movement to predict future location.

Reacting: Ability to react to a change in direction of a target and adjust crosshair accordingly.

Predicting: Ability to predict a target’s future movement patterns and location in order to adjust crosshair.


Game Specific Aim Mechanics

Recoil Pattern: Some games like Counter Strike and Valorant have specific reproducible recoil patterns for each gun. This is the direction the gun barrel moves as each shot is fired. These spray patterns can be inverted and accounted for with recoil control.

Recoil Control: Counteracting a weapon’s predetermined recoil pattern by mirroring that spray pattern with its inverse (ex. Spray goes up to the left, you must pull down to the right).
